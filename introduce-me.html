Subject: Quick intro you can pass along

Hi — I wanted to share a short, clear snapshot of what I do, in case it’s useful to pass along.

I’m a data storyteller and analyst who helps organizations make sense of messy data so they can make clearer, more grounded decisions.

My background spans global development, environmental policy, and hospitality before analytics. That mix means I’m especially strong in interdisciplinary or mission-driven settings where communication matters as much as technical skill.

Practically, I work in Python, SQL, and R, doing analysis, visualization, and applied machine learning. I’m a good fit for teams that need someone who can translate between data and people, not just build models in isolation.

I tend to thrive in nonprofits, research teams, public-facing organizations, or small companies that value thoughtful generalists over narrow specialization.

If you know of an organization or team that could use help understanding their data—or if someone’s asking, “Do you know a data person who can actually explain things?”—I’d appreciate the connection.

Portfolio: https://informedwanderer.com

LinkedIn: https://linkedin.com/in/b-johns

Email: bryan.johns@informedwanderer.com

Thanks for keeping me in mind.

— Bryan C. Johns
