About Me

Bryan C. Johns

Collaborative and results-driven Data Scientist with extensive experience in quantitative analysis, statistical methods, and visualization. Expertise in developing scalable analytics solutions and creating impactful visualizations, focused on Python, SQL, and R. Strong track record in collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver complex and impactful data insights to non-technical audiences.

I’m a hopeless generalist that fell into data analytics and visualization, focused on Python and SQL. I transform raw data into actionable insights. Earlier in life, I developed a wide array of people skills as a global vagabond, a student of international development and environmental policy, and from working in Caribbean tourism and crafting more than my share of cocktails. My combined technical and interpersonal skills are a potent force for communicating data-driven insights. Passionate about leveraging data to enhance decision-making and contribute positively to our world, I'm eager to leverage my unique blend of skills, including my analytical acumen, creative thinking, and global perspective to a challenging role in the analytics domain.

In my spare time, I love to explore the world, whether it be through mainlining nonfiction and literature, going on outdoor adventures, or simply walking in a new city or country. I find my zen in books, walks, and gardening. Beaches and mountains are my favorite playgrounds. I’ve traveled all over the world: Beijing, Bali, India (for a year), South Africa, Namibia, Istanbul, the UK, the Netherlands (for half a year), Brussels, Paris, Canada, the Virgin Islands (for three years), Mexico, Guatemala, Belize, El Salvador, Nicaragua, Costa Rica (for a year), Panama, and Peru.

My next adventure is to explore the world of data science and analytics, and to share my insights with the world. I'm excited to see where this journey takes me.
