Work With Me

Fluent in Data. Fluent in Human.

Open to both contract projects and full-time roles where data storytelling helps teams think clearly.

I help organizations communicate clearly with their data so people can act on it, not just look at it.

My background blends global development, environmental policy, and hospitality with data science and machine learning. Different audiences, different pressures, same need for clarity. I build analyses and visualizations that help teams think, plan, and act with confidence.

How My Work Helps

I design workflows and visual narratives that make data:

  • Accessible to the people who use it
  • Reproducible and trustworthy behind the scenes
  • Communicative, not just technically correct

That might be a decision-maker dashboard, a model with clean feature engineering, a written report in plain English, or a full workflow from ingestion to deployment. The deliverable fits the audience, not the other way around.

What I Build for Teams

  • Interactive dashboards and visual stories
  • Reproducible analytics pipelines
  • Machine learning for real problems
  • Data narratives for presentations or reports
  • Tools that help teams use data responsibly and confidently

Whether it’s a public-facing visualization or a quiet tool that makes internal decisions easier, I care about both what the numbers say and how people hear them.

Who I Work With

Mission-driven organizations, research groups, sustainability efforts, public-facing teams, and any group trying to make their decisions easier while preserving nuance and rigor.

Have a project, problem, or idea worth exploring? Let’s talk scope, outcomes, and next steps. Not sure exactly what you need? That’s a fine place to begin. Clarity is part of the work.
