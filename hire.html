Work With Me

Fluent in Data. Fluent in Human.

I work with teams who need their data to do more than report metrics; it needs to support clear thinking and real decisions people can act on.

What I Bring

My background spans global development, environmental policy, hospitality, and data science. Different fields, different pressures, same core challenge: making sense of complexity under real-world constraints.

I design analyses, models, and visualizations that help teams understand what’s happening, why it matters, and what to do next. The work is technically rigorous, clearly documented, and shaped for the audience that needs to use it.

How My Work Helps

I build data workflows and narratives that are:

  • Accessible to the people who use it
  • Reproducible and trustworthy behind the scenes
  • Communicative, not just technically correct

That might mean a decision-maker dashboard, a clean and interpretable model, a report written in plain English, or a full workflow from ingestion to delivery. The deliverable serves the audience, not the other way around.

What I Build

  • Interactive dashboards and visual stories
  • Reproducible analytics pipelines
  • Machine learning for real, applied problems
  • Data narratives for presentations or reports
  • Tools that help teams use data responsibly and confidently

Whether it’s a public-facing visualization or a quiet internal tool, I care about both what the numbers say and how people hear them.

Who I Work With

Mission-driven organizations, research groups, sustainability efforts, public-facing teams, and any group trying to make better decisions without sacrificing nuance or rigor.
